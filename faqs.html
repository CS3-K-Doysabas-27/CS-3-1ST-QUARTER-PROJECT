Frequently Asked Questions


Here are some questions I expect

Did the Linnean Classification end with the Two Kingdom System only?


As time passed by, new kingdom systems were made by new scientist. Now, there are currently six kingdom systems.


Did the Linnean Classification end with the 7 taxa only?


The three Domain systems is now added along with the 7 taxa. This was formulated by Carl Woose along with his colleagues. The Domain is now the most inclusive taxa.

How do I remember each taxa in the Linnean Classification without memorizing it one-by-one?


This is a very common question, especially to students who dont want to memorize each taxa with such complex words. To adress this conflict, Taxonomy Mnemonics was made. An approved mnemonic is "Did King Phillip Came Over For Good Spaghetti?" Each first letter or the words in this sentence stands a taxon in the Linnean classification. Domain Kingdom, Phylum, Class, Order, Family, Genus, Species.
